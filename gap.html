eSAIL Growth & Promotion - Instructional Design

During my time at eSAIL, I was tasked with two different projects. One was to complete a ‘capstone’ instructional video, and the second was the create a design template for the future development of eSAIL Growth & Promotion (GAP) materials.

GAP Design

The following video is an introductory video to be placed at the beginning of all eSAIL GAP instructional videos. It was created using Adobe Illustrator and After Effects. I also got to create my own GAP tutorial on Digital Accessibility!

GAP Template

The slides shown here are a snapshot of the simple PowerPoint template created for future GAP videos.

Badges & Certificate

I also designed a series of badges for students, as well as a simple award certificate, in Adobe Illustrator.

Reflection

This project gave me an opportunity to apply my design skills in a professional environment, and create content for a certain style and purpose. While I still had creative freedom, I also had constraints, forcing me to be adaptable, something I believe is very important in every workplace.
