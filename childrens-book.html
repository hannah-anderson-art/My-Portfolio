This project is still in development. I have included my current progress.

Artwork

I chose to go with watercolor as it is a fairly quick and consistent medium with the right techniques. My art process consisted of sketching out thumbnails for each piece, designing a scene in Procreate, sketching it in pencil, overlining with ink, and finally, adding watercolor! I also utilized multiple iterations to achieve pieces that I was happy with (sometimes as many as 3-4).
