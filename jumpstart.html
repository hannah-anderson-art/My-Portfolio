JumpStart - UI Design

Jumpstart falls in a blue ocean market with various opportunities. Existing apps in the market are heavily focused on maintenance tracking and are most beneficial to car owners with above-average car knowledge. The purpose of our app is to inform users about common car repairs through various forms of tutorials, translate codes used in OBD II devices, and enhance communication between car owners and mechanics.

User Research

Feel confident they’re paying a fair price when they take their car to a mechanic

Survey

From our user surveys, we learned that 36% don’t know how to fix a flat tire, 50% could not change the oil, 25% don’t know how to jump start a car, and 41% couldn’t even recognize a car engine from a photo.
